# Flirt Hair Extensions - Mobile & Desktop App
## Brand-Aligned Loyalty & Booking Platform

### Brand Identity (from www.flirthair.co.za)
- **Colors**: Minimalist palette - Black, White, Grays with subtle accent colors
- **Aesthetic**: Clean, modern, professional yet approachable
- **Tone**: Inclusive, accessible, warm, customer-focused
- **Target**: Price-conscious consumers seeking quality extensions

---

## Core Features (Required)

### 1. Hair Treatment Booking System
- **Calendar View**: Available appointment slots
- **Service Selection**: Weft, Tape, Keratin installations, Color-matching, Maintenance
- **Stylist Selection**: Choose preferred stylist or auto-assign
- **Appointment Management**: View, reschedule, cancel bookings
- **Reminder Notifications**: 24-hour and 1-hour before appointment
- **Add to Calendar**: Integration with device calendar
- **Service Duration & Pricing**: Transparent upfront costs

### 2. Product Marketplace
- **Product Categories**: Extensions (Tape, Weft, Keratin), Care Products, Accessories
- **Product Details**: High-quality images, specifications, reviews
- **Shopping Cart**: Multi-item purchases
- **Secure Checkout**: Multiple payment options
- **Order Tracking**: Real-time delivery updates
- **Wishlist**: Save favorite products
- **Product Recommendations**: Based on purchase history

### 3. Push Notification System
- **Special Offers**: Flash sales, seasonal promotions
- **New Service Alerts**: Latest treatments available
- **Appointment Reminders**: Automated booking notifications
- **Personalized Deals**: Based on customer preferences
- **Birthday Specials**: Exclusive birthday month discounts
- **Notification Preferences**: Customizable frequency and types

### 4. Referral & Rewards System
- **Unique Referral Code**: Each user gets shareable code
- **Referral Tracking**: Dashboard showing referrals and earnings
- **Tiered Rewards**:
  - 1 referral: 10% off next service
  - 3 referrals: 20% off + free product sample
  - 5 referrals: Free basic treatment
  - 10+ referrals: VIP status with ongoing perks
- **Social Sharing**: Easy share to WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook
- **Referral History**: Track who signed up and when

---

## Creative Additional Features

### 5. Virtual Hair Consultation & Try-On
- **AI Try-On**: Upload selfie, virtually try different extension styles/colors
- **Color Match Tool**: Camera-based color matching for extensions
- **Style Quiz**: Personalized recommendations based on lifestyle
- **Before/After Gallery**: User-submitted transformations
- **Save Looks**: Bookmark favorite styles for consultation

### 6. Hair Care Journey Tracker
- **Extension Lifespan Tracker**: Days since installation, maintenance reminders
- **Wash Schedule**: Track washing frequency and product usage
- **Care Tips**: Personalized based on extension type
- **Progress Photos**: Timeline of hair journey
- **Health Score**: Rate hair condition, get improvement tips

### 7. Loyalty Points System
- **Earn Points**:
  - R10 spent = 1 point
  - Booking appointment = 50 points
  - Product review = 25 points
  - Social media share = 10 points
- **Redeem Points**: Discounts on services/products
- **Tier Levels**: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum (increasing perks)
- **Points History**: Transparent tracking

### 8. Social Community Hub
- **Style Feed**: Instagram-style feed of customer looks
- **Like & Comment**: Engage with community
- **Style Challenges**: Monthly themes with prizes
- **Expert Tips**: Weekly content from stylists
- **Q&A Section**: Ask stylists questions

### 9. Stylist Profiles & Reviews
- **Stylist Portfolio**: View their work, specializations
- **Customer Reviews**: Ratings and detailed feedback
- **Availability Calendar**: Real-time booking availability
- **Direct Messaging**: Chat with stylist before booking
- **Favorite Stylist**: Quick-book with preferred professional

### 10. Subscription Services
- **Product Subscriptions**: Auto-delivery of care products
- **Maintenance Plans**: Pre-book quarterly maintenance at discount
- **VIP Membership**: Monthly fee for priority booking, exclusive discounts
- **Gift Subscriptions**: Purchase for friends/family

### 11. Educational Content Library
- **Video Tutorials**: Extension care, styling tips
- **Blog Articles**: Hair trends, care guides
- **Product Guides**: How to choose right extensions
- **FAQ Section**: Common questions answered
- **Live Workshops**: Virtual styling classes

### 12. Emergency Services
- **SOS Booking**: Last-minute appointments for urgent needs
- **Emergency Care Tips**: Quick fixes for extension issues
- **Live Chat Support**: Instant help
- **Video Call Consultation**: Remote professional advice

### 13. Personal Hair Profile
- **Hair History**: All services, products purchased
- **Allergies & Preferences**: Stored for safety
- **Measurements**: Length, color, texture preferences
- **Budget Settings**: Get recommendations within budget
- **Saved Payment Methods**: Quick checkout

### 14. Seasonal & Event Planning
- **Event Countdown**: Plan hair for weddings, parties
- **Seasonal Looks**: Trending styles for each season
- **Event Packages**: Bundled services for special occasions
- **Group Bookings**: Book with friends for group discounts

### 15. Gamification Elements
- **Daily Check-in Rewards**: Open app daily for bonus points
- **Achievement Badges**: Milestones (first booking, 5 reviews, etc.)
- **Leaderboard**: Top referrers, most engaged users
- **Spin-to-Win**: Weekly chance for random discounts
- **Challenges**: Complete tasks for special rewards
