ABOUT FLIRT HAIR

Flirt is a first of its kind one stop extension shop. Flirt offers a premium range European Virgin 100% Remy, human hair extension range. Flirt is a full service salon. Flirt is bringing hair extensions directly to the public to make it accessible to everyone. We specialise in weft, tape and keratin extension installations. We also stock our premium hair extension range, so no need for multiple salon visits. You want it, we've got it.
