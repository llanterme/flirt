FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about hair extensions

What types of hair extensions do you offer?

We offer three main types of extensions: Tape-In extensions (semi-permanent, easy to maintain), Weft extensions (sewn-in for volume and length), and Keratin bond extensions (individual strands for a natural look). All our extensions are premium European Virgin 100% Remy human hair.

How long do hair extensions last?

With proper care, our premium extensions can last 6-12 months. Tape-in extensions need to be moved up every 6-8 weeks as your natural hair grows. Weft extensions require maintenance every 8-10 weeks. Keratin bonds last 3-4 months before needing reinstallation.

Can I colour my extensions?

Yes! Our Remy human hair extensions can be coloured, highlighted, or toned just like your natural hair. We recommend having colour work done professionally at our salon to ensure the best results and to match your natural hair perfectly.

How many pieces/grams of extensions do I need?

This depends on your hair type, desired length, and volume. For tape-ins, most clients need 20-40 pieces. For wefts, 1-3 wefts depending on fullness desired. We offer free consultations to assess your hair and recommend the perfect amount for your desired look.

Will extensions damage my natural hair?

When installed and maintained correctly by professionals, extensions should not damage your hair. We use gentle application methods and high-quality products. Many clients actually find their natural hair grows healthier as extensions protect it from daily heat styling and environmental damage.

Can I wash, swim, and exercise with extensions?

Absolutely! You can live your normal life with extensions. We recommend using sulfate-free shampoo, avoiding direct heat on the bonds, and braiding your hair before swimming. Our team will provide you with complete aftercare instructions during your appointment.

Do you colour match extensions to my hair?

Yes! Colour matching is one of our specialties. We have a wide range of shades available, and we can also blend multiple colours for a perfect match. If you have balayage or highlights, we can create a custom blend that seamlessly blends with your natural hair.

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation through our website, call us at 071 617 8519, or send us a WhatsApp message. During the consultation, we'll assess your hair, discuss your goals, recommend the best extension type, and provide you with a personalised quote.
